About Kent Tools

Delivering reliable rebar processing solutions to the world.

Our Story

Built on Quality, Driven by Trust

Kent Tools is a professional manufacturer specializing in rebar processing equipment. With over 24 years of industry experience, we design, manufacture, and export a complete range of rebar tools — from tying machines and benders to cutters and threading machines.

Our mission is simple: deliver reliable, high-performance tools that construction professionals can depend on, at competitive prices, with exceptional service.

Headquartered in China, we serve distributors, wholesalers, and construction companies in over 50 countries across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. OEM and ODM services are our core strength — we bring your brand vision to life.
