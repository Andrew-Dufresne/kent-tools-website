FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What is your lead time?

Standard orders: 15-25 working days. Sample orders: 3-7 days. OEM/ODM: 25-35 days.

Do you offer OEM / ODM?

Absolutely! OEM/ODM is our core service. Custom color, logo, packaging, and specifications — we build tools to your exact requirements.

What payment terms do you accept?

T/T (30% deposit, 70% before shipment), L/C at sight, and Western Union for samples. Flexible terms for long-term partners.

What shipping methods do you offer?

Sea freight (FCL/LCL), air freight, and express courier (DHL, FedEx, UPS). FOB, CIF, or DDP available.

What warranty do you provide?

12-month warranty against manufacturing defects. Spare parts and technical support available throughout the product lifecycle.
